About

Hello, my name is Bridget Lin, and welcome to my personal film photography portfolio! I hope that by creating this website, I can remind myself of some of my favorite memories caught on camera and share them with anyone interested. The places featured include Chapel Hill, New York, San Francisco, Los Angeles, London, Florence, and Oxford. Enjoy these film pics (mostly taken on a Ricoh KR-5 Super 35mm camera).

This site is powered by Hugo, inspired by bridget.pictures, and helped greatly by Hyperplexed's YouTube tutorial.
